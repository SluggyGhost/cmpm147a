Variation in Key

Different worlds will have different generation patterns. The generation of stones and trees is directly tied to the world seed.

Signs of Life

Clicking tiles causes a house to be placed on the tile, if the tile is empty. Repeated clicks changes the color of the roof, and eventually removes the house. This information is retained; if the world seed changes, tiles that were clicked on, but had an obstacle present, may now be empty save for a house. This also works in the opposite direction; if a house tile now has an obstacle due to a change in world seed, the house will be replaced with the natural object.

Technical

Just getting the images to show up on the grid took a bit of finagling. I also had to mess with the proportions of the image to ensure that it aligned to the grid properly, which took a lot of messing around with the specific proportions. This was repeated for each sprite image, due to the variety of shapes and sizes.
